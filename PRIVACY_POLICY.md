# Privacy Policy for Empire State

**Last updated: May 18, 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how Empire State ("the App", "we", "us", or "our") handles your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

**We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information from users of the App.**

All game data (your progress, businesses, balance, settings) is stored **locally on your device** using AsyncStorage and never leaves your device.

## Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services that may collect information used to identify you:

### Google AdMob

The App displays advertisements served by Google AdMob to support free distribution of the game. AdMob may collect and use data as described in Google's Privacy Policy:
- [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- [How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services](https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites)

AdMob may collect information such as:
- Device identifiers (Advertising ID)
- IP address
- Approximate location (derived from IP)
- Information about your device (model, OS version)

This data is used by Google to serve relevant advertisements.

## Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with information, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

## Data Security

Since we do not collect or store any personal data on our servers, there is no risk of a data breach involving your personal information through the App itself. Game progress is stored locally on your device and is your responsibility to back up.

## Your Rights

You can clear all game data at any time by:
- Deleting the App from your device
- Clearing app data through your device's settings

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.
